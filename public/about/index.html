About Brad Parbs

I’m currently a Developer at Awesome Motive, where I am helping to build the easiest WordPress form plugin: WPForms. Previously Human Made, Impress, & WebDevStudios.

I like to build things on the internet.

I’m a WordPress fanatic. I’ve contributed to WordPress Core, have built a variety of custom plugins & themes, as well as a variety of tools and guides.

I have spoken at WordCamps across the United States, as well as many local meetups in Milwaukee and Chicago. I have spoken on a variety of subjects. Among those are Local Development, Building WordPress Plugins, Using the Command Line, WordPress Development, Responsive Web Design, Version Control, and more.
